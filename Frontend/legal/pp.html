Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Knowledge Café collects, uses, stores and protects your personal information while you use our learning platform.

Last Updated: July 20, 2026 Version 1.0

1. Introduction

Welcome to Knowledge Café. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal information is handled responsibly.

By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of information described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Username
  • Profile Picture (Optional)
  • Learning Progress
  • Completed Courses
  • Quiz Scores
  • Certificates Earned
  • Browser Information
  • Device Information
  • IP Address

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Track your learning progress.
  • Remember completed lessons.
  • Generate certificates.
  • Improve our courses.
  • Personalize your learning experience.
  • Provide customer support.
  • Maintain website security.

4. Cookies

We may use cookies to improve your browsing experience, remember your preferences and analyze website traffic.

You can disable cookies through your browser settings, although some features may not function properly.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration or destruction.

  • Secure connections
  • Authentication systems
  • Access controls
  • Regular security updates

6. Sharing Information

Knowledge Café does not sell your personal information.

Information may only be shared:

  • When required by law.
  • To prevent fraud or abuse.
  • With trusted service providers.
  • With your consent.

7. Learning Progress

To enhance your learning experience, we may store:

  • Completed lessons
  • Quiz attempts
  • Achievements
  • Daily streaks
  • Bookmarks
  • Certificates

8. Third-Party Services

Our platform may link to external resources including documentation, GitHub repositories, YouTube videos and other educational websites.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites.

9. Children's Privacy

If required by local law, children should use the platform under the supervision of a parent or guardian.

10. Your Rights

You may have the right to:

  • Access your personal information.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Delete your account.
  • Request a copy of your data.
  • Withdraw consent where applicable.

11. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations and improve the learning experience.

12. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any changes will be published on this page along with a new "Last Updated" date.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the Knowledge Café team through our Contact page.

Thank You

Thank you for trusting Knowledge Café with your learning journey. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a safe learning environment for everyone.
